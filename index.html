About Me

Hi, I'm Yihao (Ian) Ma, currently an MSc student in Risk Management & Financial Engineering at Imperial College London. My interests sit at the intersection of quantitative finance and data science, with a focus on how market microstructure, derivatives, and machine learning can be combined to understand and navigate real-world market dynamics.

I interned as a Data Science Intern at TikTok UK / ByteDance, conducting metric attribution analysis and user retention strategy research, and as a Derivative Trading Research Intern at Industrial Securities, developing options pricing models and backtesting delta-hedging strategies. I am a 2025 CFA Research Challenge Local Final Champion and a member of the Quantitative Engineering team at the Imperial Student Investment Fund.

Outside of finance, I enjoy building AI tools and side projects, photography and travel, and staying active through cycling and tennis. I am actively seeking opportunities in quantitative finance and data science — please feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn.
